## 3. Tier 2: Mid-Market Players

### 3.1 Vena Solutions ⭐ (Key Competitor)

**Overview:** Excel-native FP&A platform leveraging familiar spreadsheet interfaces.

| Attribute          | Details                                   |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------- |
| **Founded**        | 2011                                      |
| **Target Market**  | Mid-market (100-5,000 employees)          |
| **Pricing**        | $60K-$120K+/year (first year often $60K+) |
| **Implementation** | 2-8 months (often longer than promised)   |
| **G2 Rating**      | 4.6/5                                     |

**Top Features:**

- ✅ Excel-native interface (major selling point)
- ✅ Strong budgeting/forecasting
- ✅ Workflow automation
- ✅ Vena Copilot AI (launched April 2024)

**Weaknesses:**

- ❌ **High cost:** First year $60K+ with implementation
- ❌ **Long implementation:** Often 8+ months vs. promised timeline
- ❌ **Performance issues:** Slow loading for large models
- ❌ **Learning curve:** Advanced features require expertise
- ❌ **Limited consolidation:** Needs add-ons for full capabilities

**Customer Complaints (from reviews):**

> "Implementation took over 8 months—much longer than promised."  
> "Performance degrades significantly with large datasets."  
> "Excel reliance can be both a blessing and curse."

**Our Opportunity:**

- Beat on time-to-value (5 min vs. 8 months)
- Beat on price ($99-599/mo vs. $60K+/year)
- Offer AI-native vs. bolt-on AI

> "Learning curve is steep and the UI feels dated."  
> "Excel integration is not as robust as we hoped."

**Our Opportunity:**

- Modern, intuitive UI
- No long-term contracts
- Reliable data (no data loss)
- Cross-platform support
